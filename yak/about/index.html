About Yakima Federal

Founded in 1905, Yakima Federal Savings and Loan is a customer-owned mutual bank focused on long-term community value. Our teams serve families, homeowners, and local businesses across Central Washington with transparent guidance and practical financial solutions.

Customer-owned mission

Because we are customer-owned, decisions prioritize local members and stability rather than outside shareholder pressure.

Regional commitment

Our branch teams are rooted in Yakima Valley and Tri-Cities communities, supporting local households and small businesses.
