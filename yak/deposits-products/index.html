Deposits Products

Yakima Federal deposit offerings are structured for customers who want reliable day-to-day account access and disciplined long-term savings. Checking and debit account options focus on practical account management, while savings tiers help households organize emergency reserves and targeted goals. For customers planning ahead, CDs and IRAs provide terms that align with retirement and milestone timelines. Published deposit rates support side-by-side decision making so account holders can match liquidity needs with yield expectations and keep funds in a locally managed institution with a long history in Central Washington.
